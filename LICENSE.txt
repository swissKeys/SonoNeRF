Data used to train is a free dataset provided by ETH Zurich:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aryashah2k/2d-ultrasound-sequences-of-the-liver

The preprocessing pipeline is based on existing work:
https://github.com/facebookresearch/nonrigid_nerf

The pose estimation model is adapted from this work:
https://github.com/DIAL-RPI/FreehandUSRecon/tree/master/data/Demo
